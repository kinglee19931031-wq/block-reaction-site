《方块反应》隐私政策

最近更新日期：2025 年 12 月

本隐私政策适用于由个人开发者李晓丰（以下简称“我们”）开发和运营的游戏《方块反应》（以下简称“本游 戏”）。我们非常重视您的隐私，只在实现游戏功能、改进体验所必需的范围内处理信息。

使用本游戏即表示您已阅读并同意本隐私政策的内容。如您不同意本政策，请立即停止使用本游戏。

一、我们收集的信息

1. 本地存储的游戏数据

当前版本中，本游戏不要求您注册账号，不会收集您的姓名、身份证号、手机号、邮箱等可直接识别身份的个 人信息。

为了保存游戏进度和设置，本游戏会在您的设备本地存储如下信息：

  • 关卡进度、通关记录、无限模式历史最佳时间等游戏数据；
  • 声音开关、音量、音效类型、语言设置；
  • 新手教程完成状态、是否已查看完整教程；
  • 道具使用次数、关卡统计等功能性数据。

以上数据仅保存在您的设备本地，我们不会将其上传到自有服务器。

2. 第三方广告与 SDK

为了在游戏中展示广告，我们可能会在不同版本中集成第三方广告服务（例如 Google AdMob、穿山甲 Pangle 等）的 SDK。

这些第三方服务提供方可能会按照其隐私政策收集和处理如下信息：

  • 设备信息（如设备型号、操作系统版本、设备标识符 IDFA / Android ID 等）；
  • 网络信息（如 IP 地址、网络类型）；
  • 近似位置信息（基于 IP 或系统区域推断，一般不用于精确定位）；
  • 广告展示与点击记录，用于统计广告效果、防止作弊和安全监测。

这些信息主要用于向您展示广告、限制广告频次、统计和优化广告投放，以及进行反作弊、安全风控。

实际接入的广告服务提供方及其隐私政策链接，将会在游戏内隐私政策说明中补充和更新。

二、我们如何使用信息

  • 保存并展示您的游戏进度、成绩和记录；
  • 记住您的设置偏好（例如声音、语言），提供连续的游戏体验；
  • 在本地统计关卡完成情况，帮助我们改进关卡设计和游戏平衡；
  • 在您同意的前提下，通过第三方广告服务展示广告。

我们不会将您的信息出售给任何第三方。

三、数据的存储与安全

游戏数据存储在您的本地设备或所在平台提供的本地存储中。通常情况下，这些数据会在您卸载游戏或清除应 用数据时被删除。

我们通过最小化数据处理范围、避免收集敏感信息等方式来降低风险，但请您理解，任何网络或本地存储方式 都无法保证 100% 绝对安全。

四、儿童隐私

本游戏面向大众用户，并未专门针对 13 岁以下儿童设计。如您是未成年人用户，请在监护人同意和指导下使 用本游戏。

如您是监护人，认为我们在未经同意的情况下收集了未成年人的信息，请通过下文提供的联系方式与我们取得 联系，我们会尽快核查并删除相关数据。

五、您的权利

在适用法律允许的范围内，您对自己的数据享有以下权利：

  • 访问权：在游戏内查看自己的进度、成绩和设置；
  • 更正权：通过游戏设置修改语言、声音等偏好；
  • 删除权：通过清理应用数据或卸载本游戏的方式删除本地数据。

如果未来版本接入服务器或账号系统，我们会在隐私政策中补充相应的访问、更正和删除方式说明。

六、隐私政策的更新

随着游戏功能更新或法律法规变化，我们可能会对本隐私政策进行修改。当隐私政策发生重大变更时，我们会 在游戏内以弹窗或其他明显方式提示您，在必要时重新征得您的同意。

Block Reaction – Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jan 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game “Block Reaction” (the “Game”) developed and operated by the individual developer Li Xiaofeng (“we”, “us”). We respect your privacy and only process data that is necessary for the Game to function properly and to improve the user experience.

By using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the Game immediately.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game data stored on your device

In the current version, the Game does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, ID number, phone number or email address.

To save your progress and settings, the Game stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Level progress, completion records, best times in Endless mode and similar game data;
  • Settings such as sound on/off, volume levels, sound type and language preferences;
  • Tutorial completion status and whether you have viewed the full tutorial;
  • Item usage counts, level statistics and other functional data.

This data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to our own servers.

1.2 Third-party ads and SDKs

To display ads, we may integrate third-party advertising SDKs in different versions of the Game (for example, Google AdMob, Pangle, etc.).

These third-party providers may collect and process the following information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version, advertising identifiers such as IDFA / Android ID);
  • Network information (e.g., IP address, network type);
  • Approximate location (inferred from IP or system region, not precise GPS location);
  • Ad impression and click data for measuring ad performance, preventing fraud and ensuring security.

This information is mainly used to show ads, limit ad frequency, measure and optimize ad performance, and for anti-fraud and security purposes.

The exact list of third-party services and links to their privacy policies will be described and updated in the in-game privacy notice as we integrate them.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To save and display your game progress, scores and records;
  • To remember your preferences (such as sound and language) and provide a consistent experience;
  • To analyze level completion locally and help us balance difficulty and improve design;
  • To display ads via third-party services, with your consent where required.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Game data is stored on your device or the platform’s local storage. It is typically removed when you clear the app’s data or uninstall the Game.

We aim to minimize data collection and avoid storing unnecessary sensitive information. However, please understand that no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under 13. If you are a minor, please use the Game under the consent and supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child without your consent, please contact us and we will investigate and delete the relevant data where appropriate.

5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: view your progress, scores and settings within the Game;
  • Rectification: change your preferences (e.g., language or sound) in the settings;
  • Erasure: clear the app’s data or uninstall the Game to remove locally stored data.

If we later introduce server-side accounts or additional online features, we will update this Policy with more detailed instructions on how to exercise your rights.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example when we add new features or to comply with changes in laws and regulations. For material changes, we will notify you within the Game and, where required, obtain your consent again.
